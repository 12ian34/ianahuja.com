## ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 large leeks (about 400g), trimmed and sliced
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- 400g peas
- 750ml low-salt veg stock
- 12g (30 leaves) basil
- 3 tbsp crème fraîche
- lemon juice

## toppings

- black pepper
- olive oil
- parsley
- chilli flakes
- pancetta
- lime juice
- crème fraîche
- basil

## instructions

1. heat oil
2. add leek, add salt, cook until soft
3. add garlic and cook
4. add peas and stock
5. bring to boil and cook for 5 min
6. take off heat and cool for 5
7. add basil, crème fraîche, lemon juice
8. add salt/pepper to taste
9. blend
10. serve in bowls
11. add toppings
